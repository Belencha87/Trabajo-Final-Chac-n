About Us - Assembly of the South

We are a company that has been training professionals in the performing arts for more than 40 years. Our specialty is to provide you with the greatest number of tools to become professional in the areas of Theater, where we share our classes with teachers with many years of experience, study and work. We perform plays on and off stage, as well as non-profit street performances to entertain the residents of the southern area of Buenos Aires, The Cultural Center, which also has music classes; piano, guitar, percussion instruments, professional singing joining by our best vocalists, with experience in many Tango houses, and with an extensive experience in other styles of music; rock, soul, jazz, and urban music. We also have contemporary dance professionals, who also specialize in different musical styles ranging from rock, tango, dance, hip hop, among others. You can find in our space a new artistic makeup center, run by excellent makeup artists who have been trained in art schools, and have extensive knowledge and experience in the history of Argentine cinema and special effects.
